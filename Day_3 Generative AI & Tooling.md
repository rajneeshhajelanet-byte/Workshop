
# Day 3 – Enterprise Projects, Case Studies & MLOps Integration

## Session Vision
Day 3 connects **Agentic AI orchestration** with **enterprise adoption**. Participants will explore **compliance automation, fraud detection, database observability**, and integrate **MLOps patterns** for continuous delivery and monitoring. This day emphasizes **risk assessment, KPIs, and governance frameworks** to align technical solutions with business outcomes.

---

## Morning Session (4 Hours)

### 1. Compliance Automation
- **[KYC/AML Workflows](ca://s?q=KYC_AML_with_AI)** – automating Know Your Customer and Anti‑Money Laundering checks  
- **[Otera AI](ca://s?q=Otera_AI_fraud_detection)** – fraud detection and compliance scoring  
- **[Risk & Compliance Discipline](ca://s?q=Risk_and_compliance_in_AI)** – retail banking, commercial lending, wealth management  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Build a **compliance agent** that ingests customer data, applies AML rules, and flags anomalies.  

---

### 2. Fraud Detection
- **[Pattern Discovery](ca://s?q=Fraud_pattern_discovery_AI)** – anomaly detection with embeddings  
- **[Influence & Collaboration](ca://s?q=AIOps_influence_and_collaboration)** – predictive insights for fraud teams  
- **[Automation](ca://s?q=AIOps_automation_in_AI)** – self‑healing workflows for fraud alerts  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Integrate **Otera AI** with a fraud detection pipeline.  
- Simulate fraud scenarios and measure detection KPIs.

## Due Diligence Frameworks (Integrated Throughout Day 3)
- **Pain Points** – manual compliance checks, slow fraud detection, high MTTR  
- **Process Gap Analysis** – identify inefficiencies in compliance/fraud workflows  
- **KPIs** – fraud detection rate, compliance accuracy, MTTR reduction  
- **Risk Assessment** – regulatory risks, data privacy, model bias  

---

## Key Takeaways
By the end of Day 3, participants will:
- Build **enterprise AI agents** for compliance and fraud detection  
- Integrate **database observability** with Postgres + Datadog/CloudWatch  
- Apply **MLOps patterns** for CI/CD, monitoring, and retraining  
- Measure **KPIs** for compliance accuracy, fraud detection rate, and MTTR reduction  
- Apply **due diligence frameworks** to enterprise AI adoption
