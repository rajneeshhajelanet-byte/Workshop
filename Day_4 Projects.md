
# Day 4 – Cloud AI Services, Multimodal Agents & AIOps

## Session Vision
Day 4 explores **enterprise cloud AI services** (Azure, AWS, GCP), **multimodal agent capabilities** (Vision, Speech, Language, Document Intelligence), and **AIOps integration** for monitoring, automation, and collaboration. Participants will learn to deploy **enterprise‑grade multimodal agents** and integrate them with **observability and automation frameworks**.

---

## Morning Session (4 Hours)

### 1. Azure AI Capabilities
- **[Azure AI Foundry](ca://s?q=Azure_AI_Foundry)** – orchestration + compliance  
- **[Azure OpenAI Service](ca://s?q=Azure_OpenAI_integration)** – LLM integration  
- **[Cognitive Services](ca://s?q=Azure_Cognitive_Services)** – Vision, Speech, Language APIs  
- **[Document Intelligence](ca://s?q=Azure_Document_Intelligence)** – OCR/NLP pipelines for enterprise documents  
- **[Conversational AI](ca://s?q=Azure_Conversational_AI)** – chat/voice interfaces  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Build a **document intelligence agent** that extracts data from PDFs using OCR + embeddings.  
- Create a **voice‑enabled chatbot** using Azure Speech + FastAPI.  

---

### 2. AWS & GCP Services
- **[AWS SageMaker](ca://s?q=AWS_SageMaker_AI_agents)** – hosting fraud/risk/compliance models  
- **[Amazon S3](ca://s?q=Amazon_S3_for_AI)** – secure storage for embeddings/logs  
- **[AWS Bedrock](ca://s?q=Amazon_Bedrock_orchestration)** – orchestration with foundation models  
- **[GCP Vertex AI](ca://s?q=GCP_Vertex_AI)** – alternative orchestration platform  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Deploy a **fraud detection agent** on AWS SageMaker.  
- Store embeddings securely in Amazon S3.  
- Compare orchestration workflows between Azure AI Foundry and GCP Vertex AI.  

---

## Afternoon Session (4 Hours)

### 3. Multimodal Agents
- **[Vision Agents](ca://s?q=Vision_agents_in_AI)** – image recognition, OCR pipelines  
- **[Speech Agents](ca://s?q=Speech_agents_in_AI)** – speech‑to‑text, voice interfaces  
- **[Language Agents](ca://s?q=Language_agents_in_AI)** – NLP pipelines, conversational flows  
- **[Tabular & Transactional Data](ca://s?q=Tabular_data_AI_agents)** – structured data processing  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Build a **multimodal agent** that processes documents, speech, and tabular data.  
- Integrate with **React.js frontend** for enterprise UI.  

---

### 4. AIOps Integration
- **[Data Selection](ca://s?q=AIOps_data_selection)** – identifying relevant signals  
- **[Pattern Discovery](ca://s?q=AIOps_pattern_discovery)** – anomaly detection, fraud patterns  
- **[Influence](ca://s?q=AIOps_influence_in_AI)** – predictive insights for decision makers  
- **[Collaboration](ca://s?q=AIOps_collaboration_patterns)** – cross‑team observability dashboards  
- **[Automation](ca://s?q=AIOps_automation_in_AI)** – self‑healing workflows  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Integrate **Datadog + CloudWatch** with multimodal agents.  
- Build an **AIOps dashboard** that tracks anomalies and triggers automated responses.

## Due Diligence Frameworks (Integrated Throughout Day 4)
- **Pain Points** – siloed AI services, lack of multimodal integration, poor monitoring  
- **Process Gap Analysis** – identify inefficiencies in cloud AI adoption  
- **KPIs** – anomaly detection rate, automation success %, compliance accuracy  
- **Risk Assessment** – cloud security, data privacy, vendor lock‑in  

---

## Key Takeaways
By the end of Day 4, participants will:
- Master **Azure AI Foundry, Azure OpenAI, Cognitive Services, Document Intelligence, Conversational AI**  
- Deploy **fraud/risk/compliance agents** with AWS SageMaker + Bedrock  
- Compare orchestration workflows across **Azure, AWS, and GCP**  
- Build **multimodal agents** for documents, speech, and tabular data  
- Integrate **AIOps dashboards** for monitoring, anomaly detection, and automation  
- Apply **due diligence frameworks** to cloud AI adoption
