
# Day 5 – Governance, Security, FinOps & ADLC

## Session Vision
Day 5 integrates **governance, compliance, security, and financial optimization** into the Agentic AI lifecycle. Participants will learn **enterprise frameworks (TOGAF ADM, ADLC, FinOps, AIOps)** and practice **risk assessment, KPI definition, and due diligence**. This day ensures participants can **deploy, govern, and scale agentic AI solutions responsibly**.

---

## Morning Session (4 Hours)

### 1. AI Governance Frameworks
- **[Governance Models](ca://s?q=AI_governance_frameworks)** – compliance, audit, secure deployment  
- **[Security Layers](ca://s?q=AI_security_layers)** – IAM OIDC federation, TLS termination, payload validation, guardrails  
- **[Risk Assessment](ca://s?q=AI_risk_assessment)** – data privacy, bias, compliance risks, scalability  
- **[KPIs](ca://s?q=AI_KPIs_for_enterprise)** – MTTR, fraud detection rate, compliance accuracy, ROI  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Implement **IAM OIDC federation** for agent authentication.  
- Configure **audit logging + guardrails** for secure orchestration.  

---

### 2. FinOps & Cost Governance
- **[FinOps Principles](ca://s?q=FinOps_cost_governance)** – transparency, accountability, optimization  
- **Dashboards** – chargeback/showback, spend visualization  
- **Optimization Strategies** – workload right‑sizing, autoscaling, caching  
- **ROI Modeling** – cost vs benefit analysis for AI adoption  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Build a **FinOps dashboard** for agent workloads.  
- Simulate **cost optimization scenarios** with Kubernetes autoscaling.  

---

## Afternoon Session (4 Hours)

### 3. Agent Development Life Cycle (ADLC)
- **[ADLC Phases](ca://s?q=Agentic_SDLC_framework)** – design → build → test → deploy → govern  
- **Memory & State Management** – Pinecone, FAISS, Weaviate, Chroma  
- **Secure Tool Use** – sandboxing, payload validation, retries/fallbacks  
- **AVP Mindset** – Align Vision → Process → Technology  

**Hands‑on Lab**:  
- Design an **end‑to‑end ADLC workflow** for a compliance agent.  
- Integrate **memory persistence** with VectorDBs.

- **Architecture Vision** – define AI transformation goals

- **Technology Opportunities & Solutions** – cloud AI services, orchestration frameworks  
- **Migration Planning** – phased adoption roadmap  
- **Implementation & Governance** – compliance, monitoring, KPIs

**Hands‑on Lab**:

- Align **business architecture** with AI orchestration flows.
